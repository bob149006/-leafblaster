MOLD & MILDEW
Water runoff from clogged gutters can seep into crawlspaces causing mold and mildew growth which can damage your home’s foundation and create a health hazard.
FIRE
Unprotected gutters allow flammable debris, like dried leaves and twigs, to collect creating a potential source of ignition and flame spread.
LANDSCAPE EROSION
Pooling water from overflowing gutters can displace soil and damage plants, detracting from a home’s appearance.
INFESTATION
Clogged gutters create an ideal environment for pests, such as mosquitoes, wasps, and ants to nest.
BASEMENT FLOODING
Water can seep and flood your basement when it runs off of unprotected and clogged gutters.
LADDER SAFETY
Falls from ladders, like those used in gutter cleaning, are a leading cause of injury-related deaths in the U.S.
FOUNDATION CRACKING
Improper water control from overflowing gutters can erode soil, causing foundation materials to shift which can lead to potential structural damage.
