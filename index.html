LADDER SAFETY
Climbing ladders around clogged gutters increases fall risk.
INFESTATION
Standing water and debris create homes for bugs, rodents, and pests.
BASEMENT FLOODING
Overflowing gutters can lead to water seeping into the basement.
FOUNDATION CRACKING
Poor drainage causes soil erosion and foundation shifting/cracks.
MOLD & MILDEW
Excess moisture leads to mold growth on siding and rooflines.
FIRE
Dry leaves in gutters are fuel for ember-driven wildfires.
LANDSCAPE EROSION
Overflowing water damages soil, roots, and decorative features.
